About me


Hi there! Here is a little about myself. My name is Jesse Chua. I grew up in Toronto but lived in Hong Kong for three years. I am currently studying Computer and Cognitive Science at the Unviersity of Toronto. Cognitive Science because it attempts to explain the mental in nonmental terms (seeks to satisfy the naturalistic imperative) and I find that pretty cool to be constantly attempting to analyze, formalize, and mechanize different aspects of our cognition. Computer Science because I love solving problems and creating things that are only constrained by my imagination (and time). My most recent interest in Computer Science has been Android development.


I'm hoping to be accepted to an incubator program with my partner to develop an app over the summer that will help restaurants reach full capacity on slower nights of the week. But my interests aren't only in academia. I beatbox to express myself and play Super Smash Bros 4 to quench my thirst for competition.
